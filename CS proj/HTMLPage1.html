What is H2oh...?

People tend to forget the scarcity of water, especially the rich. This website aims to let it's readers relize how scarce water is, especially today. With this, it can hopefully persuade people to save water. The 6th SDG's goal is to ensure acess to clean water and sanitation. It is also an object of thus website to let people know how to filterwater for more acces to drinking water. Water is not only esential to health but also to peverty reduction, food scarcity, peaceand human rights, ecosystems and education.
